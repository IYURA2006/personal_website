§ 01 — A short notei.

About,
briefly.

I’m a second-year CS student who gets pulled toward the unsexy parts of building software — the testing, the evaluation, the bit where you find out whether the thing you made is actually any good.

Last year I won first place at the Huawei hackathon for an AI terminal. The night after, instead of celebrating, I stayed up reading papers — because I realised I had no idea whether what I built would still work next Tuesday. Closing that gap is the work I want to do.
